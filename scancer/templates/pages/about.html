About This Project

Scancer is a collaborative effort to increase access to the early detection of breast cancer.

Breast cancer is the most diagnosed cancer among women worldwide, accounting for 1 in 4 cancer cases. In 2020, there were an estimated 684,996 deaths from breast cancer, with a disproportionate number of these deaths occurring in low-resource settings. [1]

Survival rates for breast cancer are very high when the cancer is detected early and where treatment is available. Unfortunately, 50 to 80% of breast cancer cases are diagnosed at an advanced stage in many low- and middle-income countries, when the cancer is usually incurable. [1]
